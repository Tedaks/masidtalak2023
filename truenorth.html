True North - Book Review

It wasn't easy being an effective leader. It entails various difficulties and struggles. But every obstacle that has been faced will serve as a lesson to be learned from. as a cadet who will go through extensive training to become a future leader. The ability to recognize one's areas of strength and weakness can help one become a better leader. It is impossible to dispute that throughout the aforementioned route, there are occasionally roads with no clear path that require decision-making. One of the challenges that needed to be conquered was the effort to decide whether it was to the right or to the left. Being a successful leader requires knowing where the real north is and how to follow it while traveling. Let's lead by example and show others the way to become a good leader. There are occasionally difficult, stone-filled, muddy, and confusing pathways on roads that make them less flat and smooth. Consequently, driving on the aforementioned road required a high level of focus in order to prevent accidents and arrive at the intended location.

It stands for a person or a human being in the book True North. Each leader follows their internal compass or True North. The north was the accepted basis for direction according to a compass. When a traveler got lost, he would have relied on the map and the compass, pointing toward the north. As a result, True North demonstrates how important it is to keep on course when becoming a competent leader. By utilizing all of one's values, passions, motivations, and experiences, one can overcome problems. Your aim of being a good leader will be accomplished if you keep your eye on your True North, and your followers will follow you without hesitation because you are all traveling in the right direction. Any situation that impacts your leadership or causes you to become lost or confused will cause you to return to the right path if you always base and follow your True North.
